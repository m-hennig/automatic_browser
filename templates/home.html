Automatic Browser
Browsing for you, just like you would.
Automatic Browser is an extension for Google Chrome which learns how you browse — then sit back, relax, and it will do the work for you, automatically visiting the sites you were probably planning on looking at anyway. It might even offer some improvements.

predictive browsing.
The future means not having to tell your browser what you want to do. Automatic Browser creates a statistical model from your past behavior, logging what sites you have visited, for how long, and in what order. When you visit a site, it decides where and when you're likely to go next, and it browses that for you.
It looks out for your privacy.

Automatic Browser does not transmit urls to the server — only cryptographic tokens. No administrators can look at your data, and there is nothing for hackers to intercept. Which means your browsing history stays safe.
